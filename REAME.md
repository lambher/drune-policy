# Privacy Policy — DRUNE

**Last updated:** 18 February 2026

## 1. Introduction

DRUNE (“the App”) respects your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed when using the App.

The App is developed for iOS and uses services provided by Apple and third parties to enable multiplayer gameplay and advertising.

---

## 2. Data Collection

The developer of DRUNE does **not directly collect, store, or sell personal data**.

No account system is used outside of Apple services.

---

## 3. Game Center (Multiplayer)

DRUNE uses Game Center to provide:

- multiplayer matchmaking
- friend invitations
- player identities managed by Apple
- leaderboards or achievements (if enabled)

Game Center data is managed entirely by Apple according to Apple’s own privacy policies.

The developer does not access or store personal player information.

---

## 4. Advertising

The App may display advertisements using third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These services may automatically collect limited data such as:

- device identifiers
- basic diagnostic information
- ad interaction data

This data is processed by the advertising provider in accordance with their own privacy policies.

DRUNE does not sell user data.

---

## 5. Analytics & Diagnostics

DRUNE does not use third-party analytics services for behavioural tracking.

Basic diagnostic information may be used only for:

- crash reporting
- debugging
- improving app stability

No profiling or personalised tracking is performed by the developer.

---

## 6. Tracking

DRUNE does not perform cross-app tracking for advertising purposes.

If advertising providers require tracking permissions, users will be asked for consent through the standard iOS permission system.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

DRUNE is not specifically directed at children under 13.  
The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 8. Data Security

All multiplayer communications are handled using Apple’s Game Center networking infrastructure.

No sensitive personal data is stored on developer servers.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally.  
Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this document.
